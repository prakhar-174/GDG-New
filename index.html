Who We Are

Google Developer Groups (GDG) ABESEC is a community of developers interested in Google's developer technology products and platforms such as Android, Chrome, Drive, and Angular, Google Cloud Platform, and more.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant space for developers to learn, connect, and grow together while exploring cutting-edge technologies and building innovative solutions.
